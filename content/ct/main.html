Tofacitinib pcJIA

Study Design

DOSAGE REGIMEN STATISTICAL ANALYSIS PATIENT DISPOSITION JIA CATEGORIES

International, randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled withdrawal study

Key inclusion criteria
  • Age 2-<18 years
  • Active pcJIA, jPsA, or ERA
    • ≥5 active joints in pcJIA
    • ≥3 active joints in jPsA or ERA
  • Inadequate response or intolerance to ≥1 DMARD
  • Systemic patients without systemic features for 6 months
Key exclusion criteria
  • Systemic JIA with active systemic features
  • Persistent oligoarthritis
  • Undifferentiated JIA
  • Active uveitis
  1. Dosed at either 5 mg tablet or equivalent body weight equivalent BID with oral solution;
  2. Patients in any arm could be on NSAIDS, methotrexate, or corticosteroids.
  3. 173 patients were randomized to DB period. ACR30 =≥30% improvement in American College of Rheumatology score

ACR=acute phase reactant; BID=twice daily. DMARD=disease-modifying antirheumatic drug; ERA=enthesitis-related arthritis; JIA=juvenile idiopathic arthritis; jPsA=juvenile psoriatic arthritis; pcJIA=polyarticular-course juvenile idiopathic arthritis.
